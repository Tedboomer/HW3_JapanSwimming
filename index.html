Support the Team

The Japan Olympic Swimming Team focuses on discipline, balanced training, and smart race plans. Swimmers like Rikako Ikee, Daiya Seto, and Kosuke Hagino inspire fans with strong butterfly, medley, and freestyle races. The team goal is simple: steady practice, clean turns, and strong finishes for the flag of Japan.
